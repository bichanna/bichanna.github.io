**SLAP** is a dynamically- and strongly-typed, object-oriented programming language with C-family syntax.

- Tree-walking interpreter implemented in **Nim**
- Cross-platform CI across Linux, macOS, and Windows, plus documentation
- A SLAP-to-JavaScript transpiler in progress
